How Paper Trading Works

Entry price is fetched live from DexScreener at time of opening.
Position monitors price every 60s and tracks peak price.
Auto-exits trigger when:
• Price drops -35% from entry (hard stop)
• Price drops -40% from peak after being up +100% (trailing stop)
• Flat for 45 min with less than +30% gain (time stop)
• Whale that entered the trade exits (copy-trade positions only)
• Take profit: sells 30% at +100%, 30% at +300%, rides 40%
Manual exit: click Close in Open Positions tab anytime.
